Profile

Revolution of Knowledge and Comprehension

The IDN Junior High School (SMP) and Vocational High School (SMK) operate under the auspices of the IDN Foundation, guided by the mentorship of Mr. Dedi Gunawan, MT, CCIE. Renowned for its excellence in Information Technology (IT), SMP and SMK IDN align their curriculum with the international demands of the IT industry. Our primary focus is to equip every student, especially those in the SMK level, with skills and certifications essential for thriving in the global arena.


Supported by IDN/ID-Network and Imastudio, both of which boast an extensive client base across Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East, SMP and SMK IDN leverage this experience to venture into the realm of education. Our goal is to attract more individuals from Indonesia, preparing them to make a mark on the international stage.


Beyond formal education, we integrate "pendidikan diniyah" based on the understanding of the Quran and Sunnah. This approach not only fosters IT competencies but also nurtures the spiritual development of our students. We believe in creating well-rounded individuals who can excel both in the world of technology and in their understanding of ethical principles.
